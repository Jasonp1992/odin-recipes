Ingredients

  • 1 Pound: Spicy Italian Sausage
  • 2 Small Heads: Broccoli
  • 1 Tablespoon: Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Box: Bowtie Pasta
  • 1 Cup: Beef Stock
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Block of Parmesan Cheese (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large pot bring 4 cups of salted water to a bowl and cook pasta by the directions on the box
  2. In a 10 inch saute pan heat up a tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, crumble the spicy italian sausage into the pan and cook until sausage is no longer pink inside.
  3. Chop broccoli into florets (little trees) and add to the pan with the sausage.
  4. Add beef broth to pan, cover and cook till the broccoli is tender and bright green
  5. Add red pepper flakes and salt and pepper to taste
  6. Drain pasta and add it to the pot with the sausage and sauce and mix well.
  7. Top with freshes grated parmesan cheese, and its ready to eat!
